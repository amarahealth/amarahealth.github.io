Women’s health, supported with clarity and care.

Amara Health is a women’s health and wellness platform supporting prenatal journeys, postnatal recovery, and hormonal conditions like PCOS and PCOD.

We bring together safe movement, nutrition and mental wellbeing — guided by female specialists, delivered with consistency, not noise.

Prenatal support

Pregnancy often comes with uncertainty about what is safe, what helps, and what should be avoided.

Our prenatal programs provide structured guidance through female physiotherapists who support pregnancy-appropriate exercises at the comfort of your home — daily, alternate days, or fully customised.

Nutrition support from dieticians or nutritionists, mental wellbeing coaching, and specialised counselling such as lactation support can be integrated as needed.

Postnatal recovery

Postnatal recovery is not just about returning to routine. It is about allowing the body and mind the time and support to heal.

Our female therapists support recovery through guided movement at home, focusing on rebuilding strength, restoring stability, and easing the physical and emotional load of the postnatal phase.

Nutrition and mental wellbeing support are part of the same journey — because recovery is never only physical.

PCOS & PCOD support

PCOS and PCOD do not have a single permanent cure. They require long-term lifestyle changes that are realistic and sustainable.

Our structured programs support women over a focused period to build habits around movement, nutrition and stress management, guided by female therapists and nutrition specialists.

The goal is not short-term results, but lasting shifts that women can continue independently over time.

Looking ahead

In the future, Amara Health will also offer online training, guided routines and coaching for women who may not be able to access in-person support immediately.

We are building this carefully, with safety, trust and evidence at the centre.
