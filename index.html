Women’s health, supported with clarity and care.

Amara Health is a women’s health and wellness platform supporting prenatal journeys, postnatal recovery, and hormonal conditions like PCOS and PCOD.

We bring together safe movement, nutrition and mental wellbeing guided by female specialists, delivered at home with consistency, not noise.

Talk to a women’s health specialist

What we help with

Prenatal Care

Safe, guided movement, nutrition and mental support through pregnancy.

Postnatal Recovery

At-home physiotherapy, pelvic floor recovery and emotional wellbeing.

PCOS & PCOD

Structured lifestyle programs focused on movement, diet and consistency.

Mental & Nutritional Support

Ongoing guidance from psychologists and dieticians who understand women’s health.

How it works

1. Understand your body

We start by understanding your needs, stage and comfort.

2. Care at your home

Female therapists visit you at home with structured, safe routines.

3. Consistent support

Progress is built through consistency, guidance and mental reassurance.

Looking ahead

In the future, Amara Health will also offer online training, guided routines and coaching for women who may not be able to access in-person support immediately.

We are building this carefully, with safety, trust and evidence at the centre.
